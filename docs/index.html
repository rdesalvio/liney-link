What are linemates?

2 players are considered linemates if they:

  • Have played 600+ minutes together at any strength on the same team at the same time
  • Play the same position, i.e. forwards are not considered linemates with defensemen
  • NHL shift data only goes back to the 2012-2013 season

How to Play

Goal: Connect two NHL players through their common linemates.

Rules:

  1. You start with two target players who need to be connected
  2. Add players who are linemates with anyone already in your chain
  3. Each new player must be connected to at least one player in your current chain
  4. You win when you create a continuous path from the first to the last player
  5. You have 3 wrong guesses before the game ends
